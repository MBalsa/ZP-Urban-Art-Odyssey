About Us

Welcome to Urban Art Odyssey, your ultimate guide to exploring graffiti and street art! We are a team of art enthusiasts, urban explorers, and street culture lovers passionate about the vibrant world of graffiti and street art. Our mission is to help you discover the hidden artistic treasures in cities around the globe.

We believe that graffiti and street art are more than just colorful walls—they tell stories, spark conversations, and reflect the soul of a community. Our website offers curated guides, interactive maps, and insider tips to help you navigate urban landscapes, uncovering unique murals, street installations, and other creative works.

Whether you're a seasoned street art aficionado or a curious newcomer, Urban Art Odyssey is designed to inspire and guide your journey. Join us as we explore the art that turns city streets into living galleries, one mural at a time.

FAQ

01

Who can submit photos to the website?

Anyone can submit photos to our website! Whether you're a professional artist, an amateur photographer, or a fan who captures street art during your travels, we welcome contributions from all who share our passion for urban art. We do, however, request that submissions comply with our guidelines to ensure respect for artists and the community.

02

What guidelines should contributors follow when submitting content?

We ask contributors to ensure that the content they submit is their own or has proper permissions for sharing. We do not tolerate offensive, disrespectful, or illegal content. Additionally, we encourage contributors to respect the artists' work by acknowledging them when possible, promoting positive street art culture, and avoiding harmful stereotypes or depictions.

03

Is graffiti illegal?

Graffiti is illegal when done without permission on public or private property—it’s considered vandalism and can result in fines or other legal penalties. However, some places have designated areas or programs for legal graffiti and street art. Our website showcases artwork that celebrates creativity and respects local laws. We encourage artists to always seek permission where needed.
